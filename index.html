The Origin of Meteor
How We Began

What started as a small blog sharing favorite recipes quickly grew into a vibrant community of pancake enthusiasts. Inspired by the endless possibilities of pancake creativity, Meteor became a place to explore new flavors, artistic designs, and cultural traditions.

Over time, it evolved into a hub where beginners and experts alike could connect, learn, and celebrate everything pancakes. Today, Meteor is more than just a site — it's a universe where breakfast lovers come together to inspire and be inspired. The journey continues with fresh ideas, recipes, and stories that keep the pancake passion alive.

Meteor began as a small passion project by a group of pancake lovers who believed that pancakes were more than just breakfast—they were a way of life.
